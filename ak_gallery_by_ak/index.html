Who Are We?

We're a collective of developers who build tools we find useful and thought you might too. We don't take ourselves too seriously, but we're serious about making great tools accessible to everyone.

Our philosophy is simple: build quality tools, make them available to everyone (whether through our hosted service or GitHub), and be transparent about costs. No venture capital, no exit strategies, just sustainable tools that help people build cool stuff.
